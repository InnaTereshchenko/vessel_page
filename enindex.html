About the vessel

Ro-Ro/Rail/Passenger ferry «Greifswald» is modern cargo-passenger vessel, built in 1988 in Germany. The vessel is under Germanisher Lloyds classification supervision. Ro-Ro/Rail/Passenger ferry «Greifswald» has the certificate of the Guinness World Records, as the biggest vessel in the world in the class of PASSENGER / RO-RO / RAILWAYFERRY.

Ro-Ro/Rail/Passenger ferry «Greifswald» offers to the passengers highest level of service in comparison with other vessels and types of transport: 150 passenger places in cabins suite, semi-lux, 2-berth and 4-berth cabins with all facilities, restaurant with separate zones for smokers and not smoke passengers, a bar, reception salon, salon with video and satellite TV, a childrens game room.

Good seaworthiness of the vessel and her sizeable dimensions (length more 190m, width - 28m.) allow minimizing rolling even in storm conditions. Two cargo decks can take simultaneously aboard 50 universal rail wagons and 50 TIR.
